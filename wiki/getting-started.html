Getting Started

Your first 10 minutes

1. Download the latest nightly for your platform.

2. Extract the archive into a clean folder (avoid special characters in the path).

3. Launch the game once to generate config files.

Tips

  • Keep the engine in a short path like `C:/Games/ALEPsych/`.
  • Pair this guide with the **Installation** page to validate dependencies.
  • Join the community to share tips and starter scripts.
