Welcome to the **ALE Psych Wiki**, the central hub for documentation, learning resources, tutorials, and project guidance. This wiki provides a comprehensive overview of ALE Psych’s architecture, workflow, and practical usage.

Whether you are a new contributor, a user making a mod with the ALE Psych Engine, or simply exploring the system, you will find all essential information here.

## Getting Started

If you are new, start here.

1. **Read the Introduction** to understand the system context.
2. **Follow the Installation Guide** to set up your environment.
3. **Complete the Beginner Tutorial** to establish the foundational workflow.
4. Refer back to the **Architecture** section as you explore more advanced modules.

> If you encounter issues, the Troubleshooting section (coming soon) will walk you through known problems and solutions.
